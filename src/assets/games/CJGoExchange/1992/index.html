20th Japan-China Go Exchange, 1992

Go World does not mention the 1992 Japan-China Go Exchange, but the 1993 Kido Yearbook gives the match table and 8 games. Eight Chinese players visited Kansai Ki-in and Nihon Ki-in. They played seven rounds and won 32 and lost 24 games. Round 1 was played at Kansai Ki-in, round 2 at the Kansai headquarters of Nihon Ki-in, round 3 in Nagoya. In rounds 5, 6, and where needed 7, each played a best-of-three match.
